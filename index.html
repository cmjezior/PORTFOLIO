About

Hello there! Living in DC, I found myself diving head first into public sector marketing for enterprise IT solutions. I quickly learned the ropes of federal, state & local, and commercial marketing as I managed aspects of the Adobe Government relationship and owned 20+ complementary vendor-accounts.

Now, as a Training Developer and contractor at the Department of Justice - Asset Forfeiture Program, I collaborate with every team that takes part in the software development lifecycle. Learning how agencies operate internally has become an important takeaway. It's here that I quickly learned the IT needs of developers, engineers, & administrators and realized these needs extend to every agency.

With this insight, I'd like to continue getting the right technology into the hands of those that work to improve our digital world.
